
## What is "[taysar.com](/)"?

This website is a dirty window into [my](/essays/Who%20the%20H*ck%20is%20Taylor%20Sarrafian?) mind.

It's an attempt to jot what I'm thinking, how I'm feeling, and what I'm doing.
